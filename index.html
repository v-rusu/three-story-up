Three-Story Up

Hey there! 👋 Welcome to **Three-Story Up**.

Got a small patch of land (a 4x4 grid) and some houses? 🌆 Combine them and see the magic! Slap three of the same houses together and boom! 🎉 They level up. Easy, right? But remember, space is a luxury. So, think twice before you drop.

🎮 What to Know:

- 🏠 Drop houses onto a 4x4 grid. Easy peasy.

- 📈 Match 3 of the same houses to level them up. It feels like a mini-victory every time.

- 🎯 Got Level 5 in your sights? You got this! But if you're really brave, push for that elusive Level 6.

- 🔥 Here's a tip - match 4 or more houses for a delightful combo surprise.
